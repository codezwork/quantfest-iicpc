> THE CHALLENGE

Three Stages. One Champion.

Detailed format, eligibility, and selection criteria for each round of QuantFest 2026.

01

PRELIMS

Aug 24
Online Screen-Proctored

An offline, screen-proctored coding assessment on Codeforces featuring challenges rooted in probability, logical reasoning, and puzzle-solving. Held at 23 IITs, IIITs, BITS, ISI, NIT Karnataka, and IISc — with an online track for all other institutes across India.

Top 600 qualify →
View Details & Eligibility
  • Open to all enrolled undergraduate and postgraduate college students in India.
  • Platform: Codeforces (Screen-proctored)
  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Topics: Probability, Combinatorics, Graph Theory, DP, Logical Reasoning.
02

REGIONALS

Sep 27
In-Person

In-person at 10 IIT campuses across India. A software-based heuristic and quant simulation designed to test algorithmic thinking and domain knowledge under real-time conditions. Top 600 from Prelims compete for 75 Finals spots.

Top 75 qualify →
View Details & Venues
  • Requires in-person attendance at one of the 10 regional centers (travel not sponsored).
  • Simulation engine provided by IICPC; candidates build and deploy algorithmic agents.
  • Regional Venues: IIT Bombay, IIT Delhi, IIT Madras, IIT Kanpur, IIT Kharagpur, IIT Roorkee, IIT Guwahati, IIT Hyderabad, BITS Pilani, IIIT Hyderabad (Tentative for 2026).
03

FINALS

Oct 31 – Nov 2
Grand Finale

Three intense days at IIT Madras Research Park. Trading simulations, Jane Street competitions, HRT challenges, market-making contests, and networking with hiring professionals from the world's top quant firms.

Winner
View Details & Perks
  • Travel and accommodation sponsored by QuantFest for all 75 finalists.
  • Exclusive networking dinners with sponsors (Jane Street, Tower Research, etc.).
  • Direct fast-track interview opportunities for top performers.
  • Prize Pool: ₹8L+ distributed among overall winners and specific sponsor contest winners.
