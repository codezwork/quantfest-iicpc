ABOUT QUANTFEST

QuantFest is a one-of-a-kind national-level initiative designed to simulate real-world quantitative challenges and foster the next generation of trading and quant talent.

Spanning three rigorous stages, the competition is structured to test analytical thinking, problem-solving, and domain-specific intuition in a format inspired by leading global trading firms.

ABOUT IICPC

InterCollegiate Informatic and Competitive Programming Camp Pvt Ltd (IICPC) is a collective of like-minded students from premier institutions such as IITs and top programmers across India.

Our mission is to empower aspiring coders by fostering a culture of excellence in programming and problem-solving.

At IICPC, we conduct intensive coding camps designed to equip participants with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in competitive programming and software development. These camps not only provide valuable learning experiences but also culminate in certifications that validate participants' expertise and dedication.
